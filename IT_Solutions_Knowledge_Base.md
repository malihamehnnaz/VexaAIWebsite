## 2. Service Packages

> Three tiers allow clients to self-select. Packages are monthly retainers. Project work is quoted separately on top.

### Package Overview

| Package | Target Client | Monthly Price (SEK) | Core Services |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Starter — Get Organised** | No in-house IT at all | 1000 – 8,000 | M365 support, helpdesk, cloud backup, monitoring |
| **Growth — Get Modern** | Some IT, wants to grow digitally | 5000 – 20,000 | Starter + cloud management + GDPR + AI workshop |
| **Transformation — Get Ahead** | Actively investing in digitalisation | 10,000 – 40,000 | Growth + AI/software projects + dedicated support + SLA |

---

### Starter Package — Get Organised
*Best for: Companies with 20–200 staff and no dedicated in-house IT person*

- Microsoft 365 or Google Workspace management
- Helpdesk support (remote, business hours)
- Cloud backup and monitoring
- Monthly IT health report

---

### Growth Package — Get Modern
*Best for: Companies that have some IT but want to grow digitally*

- Everything in Starter, plus:
- Cloud infrastructure management (AWS/Azure)
- AI workshop + one automation project per quarter
- GDPR compliance review
- Dedicated account manager

---

### Transformation Package — Get Ahead
*Best for: Companies actively investing in digitalisation*

- Everything in Growth, plus:
- Custom software or AI development
- Microsoft Copilot rollout and governance
- Full digital transformation roadmap
- Priority support with SLA guarantees

## 4. Go-To-Market Strategy

### Target Client Profile
- **Size:** 20–200 employees
- **Location:** Sweden (start local/regional, expand nationally)
- **Industries:** Manufacturing, logistics, professional services, healthcare
- **Profile:** Has IT needs but no dedicated in-house IT team
- **Pain points:** GDPR worries, outdated systems, manual processes, AI curiosity
---

### Sweden-Specific Opportunities

| Opportunity | Why It Matters |
|---|---|
| GDPR compliance | Most SMEs still not fully compliant — high demand, clear ROI |
| BankID integration | Uniquely Swedish need, businesses often need technical help |
| Tillväxtverket grants | Government digitalisation grants create budget for IT projects |
| Microsoft Copilot | Being rolled out across Swedish enterprises — setup and training is a real gap |

## 5. Competitive Positioning

### Key Differentiators *(use in every sales pitch)*
- **Senior attention** — clients talk directly to the people doing the work, not account managers
- **Speed** — no corporate bureaucracy, decisions and delivery move fast
- **AI-first thinking** — modern solutions, not legacy approaches
- **Practical focus** — real ROI, not PowerPoint strategies
- **Competitive pricing** — small team = lower overheads

### Elevator Pitch
> *"We help Swedish businesses with 20–200 staff modernise their IT and adopt AI — without the cost and complexity of a big consultancy. You get senior expertise, fast delivery, and real results."*

### What to Avoid Saying
- Don't lead with technology jargon
- Don't talk about features — talk about outcomes (time saved, money saved, risk reduced)
- Don't compete on price alone — compete on value and trust

### Top Target Industries in Sweden
1. Manufacturing
2. Logistics
3. Professional services (law, finance, accounting firms)
4. Healthcare
